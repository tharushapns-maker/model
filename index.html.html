Born in Paris and raised between New York and Milan, Isabelle brings a cosmopolitan sophistication to every frame. With over a decade of experience in haute couture and editorial fashion, she has become known for her chameleon-like ability to embody diverse aesthetic visions.

Her work spans the most prestigious runways of Paris Fashion Week to the intimate pages of Vogue Italia, Harper's Bazaar, and Numéro. Beyond the camera, Isabelle is an advocate for sustainable fashion practices and mental wellness in the modeling industry.

"Fashion is not something that exists in dresses only. Fashion is in the sky, in the street, fashion has to do with ideas, the way we live, what is happening."
